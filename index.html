Mentorship

One of the main goals of New Media Club is to provide opportunities for mentorship, which strengthens our community and reinforces our bonds with our large and growing network of alumni. We create mentorship groups yearly so that our students can get to know those from different backgrounds and year levels.

We also hold mentorship events occasionally and encourage groups to meet up and get to know their peers outside of the lab. Our goal is to have students build connections stronger than just classmates and teach the next generation of students our tips and tricks.
